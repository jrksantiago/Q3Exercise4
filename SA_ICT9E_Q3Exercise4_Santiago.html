Hongkong June 6th 2017-June 9th 2017

Hongkong is mostly confused as another country of China but it is an administrative region of China it is mostly a city and is covered with thousand of buildings the streets are very busy their are also very large buildings and a beautiful beach.

It was a cold night in the summer in the beggining of June we arrived to Hongkong in midnight through plane it was my first time outside the country and I was pretty ovewhelmed I was only 8 years old and I expected for Hongkong to be pretty much the same as the country I grew up in.

It was a bright and sunny day it was my 2nd day in being in Hongkong it was the middle of summer vacation and it was very hot I was 8 years old and it was my first time to go outside of the country and we just had a big heavy breakfast it was just me, my brother, and my parents. We decided to go to disneyland on bus we went on was a tour bus and I didn't know that back then so before we went to disney land we went sight seeing and to some places that Hongkong has to offer us like malls and historical places and finally we were able to reach disney land.

What to do in hongkong : Go to Disneyland and meet disney characters, eat some food from their, go sight seeing, ride a bus, and buy items/souveniers.
